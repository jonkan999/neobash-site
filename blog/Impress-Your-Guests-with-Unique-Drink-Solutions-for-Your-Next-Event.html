Impress Your Guests with Unique Drink Solutions for Your Next Event

January 25, 2023

Welcome to the ultimate guide to impressing your guests with unique drink solutions for your next event. As the host of an event, it is important to create an atmosphere that is both enjoyable and memorable for your guests. And, what better way to do that than by offering unique and creative drink solutions? So, if you're looking to take your party to the next level, look no further, we got you. We have all the insider tips and tricks to make sure your guests are sipping on something special. Whether you're hosting a fancy soirée or a casual backyard bash, these drink solutions will take your event from ordinary to extraordinary.

Soaring Cocktail Fountains

Soaring cocktail fountain in the archipelago

When it comes to making a statement at your event, a cocktail fountain is the way to go. These towering structures will have your guests talking for months to come. Plus, they add a touch of sophistication to any event. Fill the fountain with your favorite drinks and watch as guests help themselves to a refreshing sip. And, if you're looking to keep it classy, you can always opt for a non-alcoholic fountain. Or both!

These big boys are quite in demand, so if you want one make sure to reserve in advance here today.

Frozen Margarita / Daiquiri Machine

Frozen margarita, yes please

Take your event to the beach with a frozen margarita or a daiquiri. Guests will love the cool, refreshing drinks that come from this handy machine. And, you'll love the fact that you won't have to be constantly mixing and shaking drinks. Plus, with the ability to choose from a variety of flavors, everyone is sure to find something they love. And, if you're hosting a family-friendly event, don't worry, these machines can easily make non-alcoholic versions too.

During high season these book up quite quickly so make sure to reserve yours in advance here .

Custom Labeled Beers

Custom label on a 'til death Pilsner

Adding a personal touch to your event is easy with custom labeled beers. Choose your favorite brew at Lövnäs Bryggeri and have it labeled with your event details, brand or a special special message. Guests will love taking home a special keepsake from your event. And, the best part is, it's a cost-effective way to add a special touch to your event. Don't worry, non-alcoholic options are available too.

If you like it, go for it! Reserve your batch from us in our booking service.

Whether you opt for a cocktail fountain, frozen margarita/daiquiri machine, or custom labeled beers, these unique drink solutions are sure to impress your guests. And, with the option to make them non-alcoholic, everyone can enjoy a special sip at your event. So, go ahead and take your party to the next level with these drink solutions.
