Say “I Do” to the Best Vegan Wedding Catering in Stockholm

February 1, 2023

Wedding planning can be stressful, but choosing the right catering shouldn't be. If you're looking for a vegan option for your special day, Stockholm has plenty of options to choose from. In this blog post, we'll take a look at how trendy vegan catering has become in Stockholm, the health benefits of plant-based eating, and highlight four of the best vegan wedding catering options available in the city: Vegano Catering, No Bones Catering, Lila Lotus Catering, and Bliss Cafe.

The new Vegan trend on Stockholms Catering Scene

Veganism has grown in popularity in recent years, and this trend has definitely made its way to the wedding industry. More and more couples are choosing plant-based options for their big day, and Stockholm's catering scene has responded in kind. With so many options available, it's never been easier to have a beautiful and delicious vegan wedding.

Health Benefits of Plant-Based Eating

In addition to being a compassionate choice, plant-based eating has a number of health benefits. A vegan diet is typically low in saturated fat and cholesterol, and high in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. By choosing a vegan wedding catering option, you can feel good about what you're putting into your body, and also set a positive example for your guests.

Vegano Catering

Vegan Buffee at Vegano Catering

Vegano Catering offers delicious and healthy plant-based cuisine for all occasions. Their menu features a range of dishes made with fresh, organic ingredients, and they are dedicated to creating food that is both tasty and nutritious. With a focus on local, sustainable, and seasonal ingredients, you can be sure that your wedding guests will be impressed by the quality and flavor of the food served.

This is a very popular caterer so make sure to get your orders in at Vegano here today.

No Bones

Awesome vegan buffee food from No Bones

No Bones Catering is a family-owned business with a passion for plant-based cuisine and a wealth of experience in catering. They produce their own proteins, ensuring the freshest and highest-quality ingredients in every dish. Their chefs are experts in classic hot dishes, and all meals are served in eco-friendly food boxes that are kind to the environment. With their dedication to plant-based cooking and their commitment to sustainability, No Bones Catering is a great choice for your wedding.

If you like this place don't sleep on it, make your food reservation at No Bones here before they run out.

Lila Lotus

Vegan Wedding cakes!

Lila Lotus Catering is dedicated to providing delicious and healthy vegetarian cuisine made with only the finest organic ingredients. They focus on raw food, and every dish they offer is both vegan and gluten-free, making it a great choice for those with dietary restrictions. With their commitment to quality and wellness, Lila Lotus Catering is the perfect choice for your wedding.

If you like it, go for it! Reserve your order from Lila Lotus in our booking service.

Bliss Cafe

Healthy Finger food from Bliss Cafe

Bliss Cafe is a unique dining experience located in Stockholm. As the city's first raw vegan cafe and restaurant, everything on the menu is 100% raw food. The team at Bliss Cafe believe that consuming fresh and unprocessed food leads to better health, increased energy levels, and greater overall vitality. If you're looking for a healthy and delicious wedding catering option, Bliss Cafe is a must-consider.

The clock is ticking, order food from Bliss Cafe here before they out!

Whether you're looking for classic hot dishes, raw food, or a combination of both, there's a catering option out there that's perfect for you.
