Discover the 7 Best Event Venues in Stockholm with Own Food and Alcohol Option

January 30, 2023

In this article, we will explore the top 7 event venues in Stockholm that offer the option to provide and serve food and drinks, making your event planning experience hassle-free and memorable. Whether you are hosting a business conference, a wedding reception, or a private party, these venues cater to all your needs and provide the ultimate event experience.

Why would you want venues with own food and alcohol options?

Having the option to provide and serve your own food and drinks at an event venue can save you time, money, and the hassle of coordinating with external vendors. It also allows you to personalize your menu and cater to your guests' specific tastes and preferences.

1. Skyddsrummet:

Event venue Skyddsrummet on Söder Mälarstrand

In the heart of Stockholm lies Skyddsrummet, a distinctive venue offering a versatile and adaptable event space with a fully-equipped bar and catering kitchen. Its industrial aesthetic and generous layout make it suitable for a variety of events, from corporate meetings to social gatherings. The flexible rental terms lets you bring in all the food and booze you and your guests can carry!

Popular dates are going quickly, waste no time and book Skyddsrummet here today.

2. M/S Borgila:

Event venue M/S Borgila on Söder Mälarstrand

Borgila is a sea vessel venue in the chic Södermalm neighborhood of Stockholm. With its charming rustic feel, soaring ceilings, and ample layout, combined with stunning views of the city, Borgila creates a memorable setting for events. The in-boat kitchen and bar allow you to customize your menu and cater to your guests with ease.

If you like this place don't sleep on it, book Borgila here before they run out of dates.

3. Bergrummet:

Event venue Bergrummet

Located in the heart of Stockholm, Bergrummet is a stylish and contemporary event venue that offers a unique and memorable experience. The venue's full-service bar and kitchen allow you to personalize your event's menu and provide your guests with a memorable dining experience.

If you like it, go for it! Book Bergrummet here today.

4. M/S Ambiance:

Event venue M/S Ambiance

Set on the beautiful Stockholm waterfront, M/S Ambiance is a unique venue that combines the charm of a historic ship with modern amenities. Bring all the food and booze your guests can carry and let this ship carry you around the inner waterways of Stockholm. All the while blasting out your favorite tunes.

The clock is ticking, book M/S Ambiance here before the ship has sailed!

5. Generator:

Event venue Generator

Generator is a trendy and versatile event venue located in Stockholm's fashionable Vasastan neighborhood. With its modern design and spacious layout, Generator is the perfect setting for a wide range of events, from corporate meetings to social gatherings. The venue's on-site kitchen and bar options allow you to customize your event's menu and serve your guests with ease.

Popular dates are going quickly, waste no time and book Generator here today.

6. Westmanska Palatset:

Event venue Westmanska Palatset

Westmanska Palatset is a grand and elegant event venue located in Stockholm's fashionable Östermalm neighborhood. With its spacious and well-appointed event spaces, Westmanska Palatset is the perfect setting for large-scale events, from weddings to corporate conferences. The venue's own catering service and bar options allow you to personalize your event's menu and provide your guests with a memorable dining experience.

If you like it, go for it! Book Westmanska Palatset today.

7. H62:

Event venue H62

H62 is a sleek and contemporary event space located in the fashionable Södermalm neighborhood of Stockholm, near Zinkensdam station. With all the necessary amenities for birthday parties, corporate events, and weddings, it's the perfect venue for any occasion. And with flexible rental terms, you can save on expenses by bringing your own liquor and food for an unforgettable celebration.

If you like this place don't sleep on it, book H62 here before they run out of dates.
