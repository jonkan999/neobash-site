Creative variations on classic drink canape for catering

March 23, 2023

Drink snacks are an essential part of a successful event catering. Whether you're planning a corporate party, a wedding reception, or a private gathering, the small bites served with drinks are a must-have to keep guests happy. But why not make it even more fun by adding a modern twist to some of the classic canape? There are no hard rules here, but we try to think of adding a lot of color for visual effect. And also throw in a lot of bold flavors, to try and delivery a full flavor experience in a small package. Below we have listed three creative variations that we like to work with when we are throwing a bash.

1. The Bruschetta

Our choice of baked tomato and queso fresco or feta

We have a real banger of a crowd pleaser pizza that goes by "Puttanesca mess" which we have repackaged in to a bruschetta on occasion. It really packs a flavor punch and is super easy to make. This is how we do it, but you can of course simplify on your own: Oven bake cherry tomatoes in olive oil and salt until semi dryed. Assemble that with chopped up olives and capres on any toasted bread slice. then we top off with chopped parsley and our killer mayo made on parmesan and roasted garlic. Trust us, this one is great and also completely vegetarian, so no optionallity headache there.

2. Matcha punch in a drink fountain

The matcha drink fountain we had at our release party

Basically, any drink works in a drink fountain. However, in this case, we chose to make it with matcha tea that we got from YOKO. Trust us, it's great! We mixed two parts matcha with 1 part gin and topped it off with lots of freshly squeezed lime and lemon. Why not add some antioxidants to your cocktail, right?

You can rent drink fountains from our friends here.

3. Label your own beer

Here we have draped our own branding over Lövnäs' branded white label beer

Our buddies at Lövnäs Bryggeri supply us with white-labeled beer that we can brand ourselves, and they can do it for you too! Here, we have a canned pale ale that we put our logo on for our release party. We showcased it in a silver-on-silver-on-silver setting, which was nicely captured by one of our partygoers here.

You can find out more about their own labeled beer here. Or contact us directly for help with branding and etiquetting.

4. Frozen Margarita

It's much less complicated than you might think. If you have a slushie machine, you simply need to pour in the ingredients. We prefer to use a mixture of 6 parts lime, 3 parts tequila, and 1 part triple sec (Cointreau works fine!). Let it sit for a couple of hours and then have your guests line up with salt-rimmed glasses in hand.

Here is our favorite supplier of slushie machines for Margaritas.

5. Sourbeer raspberry floater

We can't guarantee that your floater will look exactly like it does in the picture!

Surprisingly delicious, we have made this on several occasions now, and it always turns out to be a crowd-pleaser. Mix one part vanilla ice cream with two parts raspberry-flavored sour beer, which we, of course, get from our partner supplier, Lövnäs Bryggeri, and top it off with raspberry coulis. If you have access to paper cups, that's an excellent, fun way to serve it for an authentic American vibe.

You can find out more about Lövnäs and their assortment here.

And there you have it, partyplanners – our 3 variations on canapes. All of this is targetted towards the DIY party planner, but ofcourse we can help you out and supply a full service catering experience for you.
