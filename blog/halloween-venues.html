Our top 3 picks of Party Venues in Stockholm for a Spooktacular Halloween Bash

August 22, 2023

Looking to host the most memorable Halloween party in Stockholm? Your search ends here. We've handpicked three exceptional venues that will set the stage for a spooktacular night, complete with the eerie ambiance and ample space you need. Get ready to discover the top spots where you can celebrate with your own drinks and experience Halloween like never before. Starting from small and cozy to big and spacious, we have something for everyone. Here we go!

1. Matron at Cow Pow - Vasastan's Enigmatic Gem

Event venue Matron at Cow Pow in Vasastan

First on our list is Matron at Cow Pow, situated in the heart of Vasastan. This captivating venue is perfect for hosting an intimate gathering of around 50 guests. Matron is situated in a legendary music studio and provides a unique backdrop for your Halloween festivities. What's more, you have the freedom to bring your own beverages, ensuring a personalized touch to your event.

If you like this place don't sleep on it, book Matron here before they run out of dates.

2. M/S Borgila - Nautical Mystique Meets Halloween

Event venue M/S Borgila on Söder Mälarstrand

Sailing into our second spot is MS Borgila, a remarkable boat venue that can accommodate up to 150 guests. Transform this nautical setting into a ghostly ship, where every corner exudes an air of mystery. Whether you envision a haunted sailor's haunt or a chilling maritime masquerade, MS Borgila provides the canvas for your Halloween vision. And yes, you can raise a toast with your preferred drinks on board!

This is a popular Halloween venue, so book Borgila here before it's too late.

3. Skyddsrummet - Subterranean Enchantment:

Event venue Skyddsrummet on Söder Mälarstrand

Our final pick is the enchanting Skyddsrummet, a unique cave venue that offers both intrigue and comfort. With a capacity for 200+ guests, this subterranean space provides an unforgettable setting for your Halloween gathering. Immerse yourself in the cave's natural beauty while reveling in the festivities above ground. And of course, you're welcome to bring your own drinks to enhance the celebration.

Popular dates are going quickly, waste no time and book Skyddsrummet here today.

But that's not all. These venues are more than just spaces; they're our partners in creating a seamless Halloween experience. At Neo Bash, we offer comprehensive support, from catering and service to decor. Our mission is to ensure that every aspect of your Halloween party aligns with your vision, making it an occasion that leaves a lasting impression.

BYOB and whatnot

All the above venues are completely self service, meaning that you can bring your own food and alcohol as you please. Please reach out to us if you need any additional help with catering, service or decor. We are here to help you create the best event possible.
